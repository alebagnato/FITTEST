ABCI tool: abciExample

This is intended as an example of ABCI developers. It shows how an instrumentation tool is composed from ASIL. This tool will inject a call trace(...) at the beginning of every method, and just before retun-instructions. In ActionScript, the function trace(s) will print the string s into the Flash's log file. (You need to set up your Flash to enable its logging; see its documentation)

Usage:see here. The -i option will do the trace(..) injection as mentioned above.

The easiest way to alter abciExample is by changing the behavior of -i. In other words, changing the behavior of its instrumentation feature. All other functionalities such as statistics and pretty printing will be inherited.
